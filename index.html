How we create value

Four levers, applied from day one

We do not wait for a future event to create value. The moment a company joins Arrax, all four levers go to work.

Capital

We deploy long-term, patient capital and acquire businesses outright. No clock, no forced exit.

AI & Automation

From day one we apply agents and automation to the work that drains every operating company, then reuse it everywhere.

Operational excellence

Operators, not spreadsheets. We run the businesses, raise the standard, and remove the friction that limits them.

Long-term ownership

We are the last owner, not the next one. Teams and customers get continuity, not a flip.

The platform

One platform. Every company benefits.

Behind Arrax is a single AI and automation platform that every portfolio company runs on. It learns each business, modernizes its systems, and turns the work into reusable assets. Each acquisition makes the platform stronger, so the next one is faster and cheaper.

  • Connectors, agents and playbooks built once, reused across the whole portfolio
  • Legacy systems modernized with AI rather than rebuilt from scratch
  • Operations run with shared monitoring, security and cost discipline
  • A compounding advantage that competitors starting from zero cannot match
